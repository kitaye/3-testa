TEST IQ
The IQ test measures the intelligence level including short-term memory, analytical thinking, mathematical ability and spatial recognition.
It does not attempt to measure the amount of information you have learned but rather the capacity to learn.
TEST PAEI (Izchak Adizes)
The PAEI Management Model, developed by Dr. Izchak Adizes, is a popular method method for assisting people in examining their
“work personality” and which role they play within a team.
TEST MBTI
The Myers–Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) is an introspective self-report test with the purpose of indicating differing psychological preferences in
how people perceive the world around them and make decisions.
